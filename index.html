I am a second-year Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Ding Zhao in Safe AI Lab. I also work closely with Prof. Bo Li. I received my bachelor degree from Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in Jul, 2018.

I am interested in deep generative models, causal inference and data-driven simulations. Especially, I focus on understanding, reasoning and generating safety-critical scenarios for training and evaluating intelligent robots.
